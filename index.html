Personal Bio

I have been working in the Environmental Sector for the 10 years both on the regulatory and private/commercial side. For the last 6 years I've been involved with the Technologies division of the company I currently work for. We've designed and utilize a proprietary remote real-time monintoring system used on major construction and remediation sites. I have been involved as a Project Manager and a Product Manager for the division. Throughout my time at my current company I've gained experience on the development side by working closely with programmers and writing some of my own code. I have experience in writing .bat files, vbs files, Excel macros, html, and am currently completing a full-stack developer course through Rutgers University.

There's a wide variety of monitoring instruments out there for people to use and each of them has their own code to make them function.
